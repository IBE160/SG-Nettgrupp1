---
story: As a Customer, I want to be able to add and manage items in my shopping cart so that I can prepare my order.
epic: Ordering
story_points: 8
priority: Must-Have
acceptance_criteria:
  - The user can add a product to the shopping cart from the product catalog or the product detail page.
  - The user can remove a product from the shopping cart.
  - The user can change the quantity of a product in the shopping cart.
  - The shopping cart must display the subtotal for each item and the total for the entire order.
  - The cart icon in the header must show the number of items in the cart.
  - The shopping cart page must be responsive and display correctly on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
---
