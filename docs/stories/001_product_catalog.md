---
story: As a Customer, I want to be able to see a catalog of products so that I can browse the items available in the store.
epic: Product Management
story_points: 8
priority: Must-Have
acceptance_criteria:
  - The application must display a list of all products in the "Cigars and Accessories" category.
  - Each product in the catalog must display its name, price, and a small image.
  - The catalog must show the current stock level for each product (e.g., "In stock: 10").
  - The user can click on a product to navigate to a more detailed product view.
  - The product catalog must be responsive and display correctly on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
