Product Catalog Directions

Direction 1: Grid Focus (Visual Browsing)

[Placeholder: Grid of large product images, minimal text]
  • **Layout:** Prominent grid of product cards, large images.
  • **Visual Hierarchy:** Images are primary, product name/price secondary.
  • **Interaction:** Hover effects on cards, quick-add-to-cart button on hover.
  • **Visual Weight:** Balanced, clean, with focus on product imagery.
  • **Content:** Minimal text on cards, details on click.
  • **Personality:** Engaging, visually driven, modern.
Notes: Best for users who prefer to browse visually and are drawn by product aesthetics.

Direction 2: List Focus (Information-Rich)

[Placeholder: List view with product image, name, price, origin, strength, etc.]
  • **Layout:** List view with more product details visible (e.g., origin, strength).
  • **Visual Hierarchy:** Product name and key attributes are prominent.
  • **Interaction:** Expandable sections for more details, clear "Add to Cart" buttons.
  • **Visual Weight:** Denser, but organized for scannability.
  • **Content:** More text, structured information.
  • **Personality:** Informative, efficient, for users who know what they're looking for.
Notes: Ideal for users who prioritize detailed information and efficient comparison.

Direction 3: Curated Carousel (Guided Discovery)

[Placeholder: Large hero carousel with featured products, followed by smaller grids/lists]
  • **Layout:** Large hero section with featured products in a carousel, followed by smaller grids/lists.
  • **Visual Hierarchy:** Featured products are dominant, guiding attention.
  • **Interaction:** Smooth carousel navigation, clear calls to action for featured items.
  • **Visual Weight:** Dynamic, engaging, with clear focal points.
  • **Content:** Highlights key features of curated items.
  • **Personality:** Welcoming, guiding, for users seeking recommendations.
Notes: Good for showcasing promotions or guiding new users to popular/recommended items.

Product Detail Page Directions

Direction 4: Immersive Detail (Storytelling)

[Placeholder: Large product image, rich descriptive text, elegant layout]
  • **Layout:** Large hero image/video, rich descriptive text, elegant typography.
  • **Visual Hierarchy:** Product imagery and storytelling are paramount.
  • **Interaction:** Smooth scrolling, subtle animations, clear "Add to Cart" with quantity selector.
  • **Visual Weight:** Spacious, premium feel, focus on product experience.
  • **Content:** Detailed descriptions, origin stories, tasting notes.
  • **Personality:** Luxurious, engaging, for enthusiasts.
Notes: Aims to create an emotional connection with the product through rich content.

Direction 5: Concise Overview (Efficient Purchase)

[Placeholder: Key info (image, price, stock, add to cart) above fold, concise details below]
  • **Layout:** Key information (image, price, stock, add to cart) above the fold, details below.
  • **Visual Hierarchy:** Purchase-related actions are most prominent.
  • **Interaction:** Quick quantity adjustment, clear "Add to Cart" button.
  • **Visual Weight:** Clean, functional, direct.
  • **Content:** Bullet points for key features, concise descriptions.
  • **Personality:** Efficient, direct, for users ready to buy.
Notes: Optimized for quick decision-making and adding to cart.

Please review these directions. You can choose a favorite by number, combine elements (e.g., "The catalog layout from #1 with the product detail from #5"), or request modifications.
